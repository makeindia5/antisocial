Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026

Welcome to Antisocial ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our Service:

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your phone number for OTP authentication and your chosen profile name.
  • Media & Files: If you grant permission, we access your device's camera and photo gallery to allow you to send images, videos, and set profile pictures.
  • Messages: Your chat history (text, media) is stored securely on our servers to enable communication between users.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (model, OS version) to troubleshoot issues and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and allow you to log in securely.
  • To facilitate real-time messaging and group chats.
  • To enable features like image sharing and profile customization.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera: To take photos and videos directly within the app.
  • Storage (Read/Write): To upload photos/videos and save received media to your device.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

How to Request Deletion:

  1. Email Request: Send an email to our support team at support@antisocial.app (Replace with your actual email).
